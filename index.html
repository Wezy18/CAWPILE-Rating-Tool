CAWPILE Book Review Tool

The CAWPILE book rating system is a structured, category-based method for reviewing books, originally created by G from Book Roast. The acronym stands for Characters, Atmosphere, Writing, Plot, Intrigue, Logic, and Enjoyment, with each category rated on a scale from 1 to 10. These scores are then averaged to produce an overall rating out of 10, which can be translated into a star rating. It’s designed to encourage thoughtful reflection on specific aspects of a book, rather than relying solely on gut feeling. This tool is using SusanLynKnits explanation as a foundation due to her redefining or expanding the category descriptions to more accurately reflect what matters most to readers when evaluating a book.
